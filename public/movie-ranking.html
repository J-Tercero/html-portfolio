A Night at the Movies

A List of movies worth relaxing to on a rainy night by Justin

The Goonies

The Goonies is a classic movie combing the imagination of Steven Spielberg and the story telling of Richard Donner. an original tale of a group of misfits that call themselves "The Goonies" that discovers a pirates treasure map that launches them on an epic quest. Full of fun, laughter, excitement, and adventure The Goonies is great for a rainy day at home with the kids!

The Shawshank Redemption

An absolute masterpiece by Steven King. The Shawshank Redemption follows the story of Andy Dufrene, a local banker in 1947 convicted of killing his wife and his story in prison. Get ready to be taken on a tale or friendship, fraud, murder, Rita Hayworth, and Mexico. By the end of the movie you'll be reflecting in your soul on the meaning of life and happiness. Get busy living or get busy dying, but you must watch this movie!

The Rock

Action at it's finest! Don Simpson and Jerry Bruckheimer together have put together some of the best films of our time and The Rock is no different. Tired of perceived mistreatment from the government, a band of rouge Marines take over Alcatraz Island and threaten to launch weapons of mass destruction if their demand for justice are not met. A chemical weapons expert from the FBI along with Navy SEALs are dispatched to retake the island and save San Francisco from catastrophe.
